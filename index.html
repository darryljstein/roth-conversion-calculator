Planning assumptions inside this prototype
Tax calculations are modeled using a simplified 2026-style bracket framework for MFJ, Single, and Head of Household. IRMAA is approximated with filing-status-specific thresholds. Social Security taxation is estimated using provisional-income rules. RMDs use the Uniform Lifetime Table style factors with current age-based start rules. When a spouse death event is modeled, the survivor receives the higher of the two Social Security benefits and the filing status switches to Single in the year after death. This is a planning prototype, not tax, investment, Medicare, or legal advice.
